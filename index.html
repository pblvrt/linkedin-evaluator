Free · A sales tool, not a personal-brand audit

Turn your LinkedIn into a sales machine.

Your profile is a sales page that 200+ buyers see every month. Most look like CVs. This audit scores yours across the 7 levers that turn visitors into replies, then rewrites the parts that aren't pulling weight — so the next prospect who lands on it actually books the call.

01Set up Claude Code (one-time)

Claude Code is the AI tool that can use your computer for you. You'll use it for way more than this audit — but if you don't have it yet, grab it here: claude.com/claude-code. Takes 2 minutes.

Then run this command once to let it talk to your browser:

claude mcp add chrome-devtools npx chrome-devtools-mcp@latest

Open Chrome, log into LinkedIn like you normally do. That's the setup. You'll never do this part again.

02Run the audit

Paste this into Claude Code. Swap in your LinkedIn URL:

Audit my LinkedIn profile using the framework at https://raw.githubusercontent.com/pblvrt/linkedin-evaluator/main/audit.md — my profile is https://linkedin.com/in/YOUR-HANDLE

Claude opens your Chrome, scrolls your profile like a real person would, and writes the audit while you watch. ~2 minutes.

Headline

The first thing every prospect sees. Names the buyer, the outcome, why you.

Photo + banner. Your storefront. Default LinkedIn blue costs you replies.

About

Mini sales page above the "see more" fold. Hook, proof, CTA — not a CV.

Featured

Case study + lead magnet + best post. Where warm visitors actually convert.

Experience

Outcomes with numbers. Builds buyer-credibility, not recruiter-credibility.

Skills

Top 3 line up with the headline. Endorsed by people in your buyer's world.

Content

How prospects find you. Cadence, themes, original substance. No ghost towns.

+ Rewrites

3 new headlines, a fresh About opening, banner copy, one post idea for this week.

Then it asks which buyer you actually want to attract and regenerates the rewrites for that buyer specifically.

04Built for sales, not personal branding

Most LinkedIn coaches optimize for likes and follower count. Useless if you sell. This audit optimizes for the part of the funnel they ignore: the prospect who lands on your profile after a cold DM, a comment, or a referral — and decides in 8 seconds whether to keep reading or close the tab. Every line of feedback maps back to one thing: does this sell, or does this just look nice?
